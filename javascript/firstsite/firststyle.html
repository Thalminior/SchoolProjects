Brief timeline of events in Veridin

Aspis calendar dates

~600 pre Ascension, rise of god king Magrath

  • 400-600 year reign ending in bloodbath and mass killing upon his alters
  • Not much known of Magraths reign many texts destroyed and knowledge erased

1st year of Ascension

  • Aspis "defeats" Magrath in "great temple basin"
  • Aspis ascends to godhood
  • Aspis creditied for creating human race

2-2436 Ascension

  • "Saints of Aspis" lead human race to flourish
  • humans spead south and west creating aspis high city south of great temple
  • church seals off great temple basin only high clergy allowed pilgramige
  • Aspis military religion becoms world power its borders fill all of western contenent
  • growth limited by great central desert

The years of Death and rebirth

2436 Ascension - The year of death

  • Magrath wakes; he escapes his imprisonment and resumes his campaign of death.
  • Aspis high (capital) city stricken with demonic plague, first to fall to undead.
  • undead hordes march on world under Magraths banner - war of ash and bone
    • Undead hordes coordinated by death lords
    • raising all slaughtered to fight as undead
    • 90% of known world consumed within one year

2437-2438 Ascension (Aspis calendar)
1st year New Era (Magi calendar) - The year of Rebirth

  • Bright flash in sky accompanied by light eminating from within ground,
    • all undead gone afterwards, crumbled to dust.
    • Many strange people of various non-human races found on battlefields across world
    • those spoken to talk of returning home to greet their new empress
  • Magi city (the cradle) appears, filling Aspis ascension temple crater

Era of the Magi

2-50 New Era (Magi calendar)

  • New Empress proclaims herself for the people of the Magi
  • Magi aid in rebuilding of other kingdoms
  • Magi people reveal many lost Magical arts and have great powers

50-200 New Era (Magi calendar)

  • Aspis officially declares war with Magi
  • Aspis loose high city to Magi after attacking Magi citizens on pilgrimage
  • Gilmore government begins to destabilize
  • Magi begin resurfacing lost cities in desert
  • Magi have sealed off all access to Redwall
  • Aspis begin losing ground to Magi
  • death of Gilmore high Chancellor Uguth Ington begins internal civil war
  • Gnomeian steps into Gilmore power vacuum claiming chancellorship and lordship of Country (with aid of friends and quiet Magi backing.)
  • Gilmore becomes a Magi state, keeping much of its independence and sovereignty (negotiated and handled by Gnomeian
  • Gnomeian rules for many years

200-700 New Era (Magi calendar)

  • Many new races and cultures that had been isolated found and brought into Magi empire
  • Barren desert now fertile grasslands
  • Magi declare official war on Aspis push them deep into their homelands

700-740 New Era (Magi calendar)

  • Magi begin “recruiting” to “free” Redwall
  • Magi expansion into great desert almost complete. Some southeastern areas still “under supervision”

740-745(present) New Era (Magi calendar)

  • Redwall time bubble collapses Magi begin clearing process (740 New Era)
  • Redwall open for new settlers (743 New Era)
  • Magi empress announces successor as: her daughter Aliya, and intention to step down from throne (743 New Era)
  • New Empresses coronation (745 New Era)
